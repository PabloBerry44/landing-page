Avène Suncare SPF 50+

Very high sun and anti-ageing protection for sensitive skin.

Anti-ageing Suncare Tinted SPF 50+ sun protection and anti-ageing treatment, contributes to the prevention of photo-ageing of the sensitive skin of all women who wish to preserve their beauty. Its tinted texture moisturises the skin, evens it and leaves it soft and supple.
At the heart of its formula, is Ascofilline, an anti-ageing* active ingredient, and the exclusive Sunsitive® protection complex developed by Pierre Fabre Research and composed of:
  • A patented filter system containing only four sun filters, for very broad and stable UVB-UVA protection and optimal skin tolerance.
  • Provitamin E (Pre-tocopheryl), a powerful antioxidant, to protect cells against free radicals.
  • Avène Thermal Spring Water known for its soothing, anti-irritant and softening properties.
Practical, its pump tube allows you to easily dose the right amount of product to be applied.

A few words from our expert

Very high sun protection for sensitive skin enriched with anti-ageing active ingredients to protect skin from photo-ageing.

APPLICATION

Protect yourself from the sun and enjoy it more safely

When to use?

As often as necessary

How to use?

Before exposure to the sun, apply 6 pumps to the face and neck. Reapply frequently to maintain protection, especially after sweating, swimming, or towel drying.

An expert tip

For easy application, spread over your fingertips and dab onto your face and neck. Then smooth.
